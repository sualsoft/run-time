About Us

At RunTime, we specialize in recruiting for a wide range of technology sectors, including Automotive, Medical, Telecommunications, Industrial Control, IOT / M2M, Product Design, Embedded Systems, Firmware Development, Audio, Defense Systems, Electronic Engineering, and Hardware Design. We deeply understand the skills and experience needed to succeed in these industries, and we are committed to finding the best candidates for your open positions.

We know that job specifications can be complex and difficult to define, which is why we take the time to understand your specific needs and requirements. Our team will ask you questions about your projects, the problems you are trying to solve, and your team and company culture. We believe that by clearly understanding your needs, we can provide you with the best candidates for your open positions.

We understand that job specs can often feel like wishlists, and we are here to help you define the essential skills and experience you need. Our team will work with you to help you identify the most important requirements for the role and to ensure that we find the most qualified candidates for the job at hand.

Our team moves quickly to provide you with a good candidate flow of qualified candidates for your open positions. We understand that time is of the essence when it comes to hiring, and we are committed to providing you with the best candidates as quickly as possible. In return, we request a prompt response from you so we may secure the candidates and get them through the interview process as quickly as possible.

When working with candidates, we guarantee an honest and quick response in complete confidence. We know that job hunting can be stressful and challenging, so we are committed to providing candidates with the support they need to succeed. We handle interviews with professionalism and speed, and we help candidates through salary negotiations, pre-interview coaching and resume advice.

Our success is built on strong relationships with our clients and candidates. Our team is committed to providing exceptional service and support, and we are always looking for ways to improve our processes and services. We take the time to get to know you and your organization, so we can offer personalized service and support tailored to your needs.

In addition to our recruiting services, we offer a comprehensive screening process to ensure that we only present candidates who are the best fit for your organization. This includes a review of their skills, experience, and cultural fit.

For candidates, we offer a range of services to help you find the right job. We provide career coaching, resume advice, and interview preparation to help you stand out from other candidates. We also work with a range of companies across different industries, so we can offer you access to a wide range of job opportunities.

If you are looking for the best recruiting and staffing agency to help you find the most qualified candidates for your open positions, look no further than RunTime. Our team of engineers turned recruiters and researchers has a deep understanding of the technology sector and is committed to providing exceptional service and support to our clients and candidates. We take a unique approach to candidate sourcing and are committed to moving quickly to provide you with a good flow of qualified candidates. We believe that by working together, we can help you achieve your goals and succeed in your business.

Electronics Engineers

Mechanical Engineers

FAE Engineers

FAE Engineers (Field Application Engineer) provide technical support to customers, helping them integrate complex products into their systems.

Firmware Engineers

Firmware Engineers design and develop low-level software that controls the hardware of electronic devices, from smartphones to autonomous vehicles.

Avionics Engineers

Avionics Engineers design, develop and test electronic systems used in aircraft, including navigation, communication, and flight control systems.

Control Systems Engineers

Control Systems Engineers design, develop and implements automated control systems, such as those used in manufacturing and transportation.

Software Engineers C/C++

C/C++ Software Engineers design and develop software using the C and C++ programming languages, used in systems programming and embedded devices.

Machine Learning Engineers

Machine Learning Engineers designs and develops systems that can learn from data and make predictions, used in fields such as AI and data analysis.

Engineering Managers

Engineering Managers oversees technical projects and teams, manages budgets, allocates resources, and ensures successful project delivery.

FGPA Engineers

FPGA Engineers design, implement, and test custom digital circuits using FPGAs, and collaborate with other engineers to optimize system designs and troubleshoot issues during the process.

Full-Stack Engineers

Full-Stack Engineers are responsible for designing, developing, and maintaining both the front-end and back-end components of web applications, ensuring they function smoothly and efficiently from end to end.
